<p><ul><li>Fiber Laser</li><li>CO₂ Laser</li><li>Solid-state Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser marking equipment market is primarily categorized into three types: Fiber Laser, CO₂ Laser, and Solid-state Laser. Fiber Lasers utilize optical fibers doped with rare earth elements, ideal for marking metals and plastics with high precision. CO₂ Lasers use a gas mixture, excelling in marking non-metal materials like wood, glass, and acrylic. Solid-state Lasers, often using crystals, provide versatility for a range of applications, offering effective marking on diverse materials with varying levels of speed and quality.</p></p>

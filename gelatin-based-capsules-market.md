<p><p>The gelatin-based capsules market is primarily divided into two types: soft gelatin capsules and hard gelatin capsules. Soft gelatin capsules are hermetically sealed, allowing for liquids or oils to be encapsulated, making them ideal for vitamins and herbal supplements. Hard gelatin capsules consist of two pieces that can contain powdered or granulated substances, commonly used for pharmaceuticals. Both types offer advantages such as enhanced bioavailability, convenience of dosage, and improved taste masking, catering to diverse consumer needs in the health sector.</p></p>

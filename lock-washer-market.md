<p><p>The lock washer market encompasses various types that enhance fastening reliability. Internal star washers feature serrated edges designed to grip against the fastener and substrate, preventing loosening from vibrations. External star washers, having similar serrations but positioned externally, provide added grip to the base material. Split washers, or spring washers, utilize a wave-like structure to exert tension and resist loosening. Other lock washers may include specialized designs for specific applications, ensuring effective fastening across diverse industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor Decontamination System market comprises two primary types: Vaporized Type and Fogging Type. The Vaporized Type utilizes pure hydrogen peroxide vapor to penetrate surfaces, effectively eliminating pathogens without leaving residue. In contrast, the Fogging Type disperses a mist of hydrogen peroxide solution, targeting larger areas and surfaces while maintaining a thorough decontamination process. Both methods are widely used in healthcare, pharmaceutical, and food processing industries for creating sterile environments by effectively reducing microbial contamination.</p></p>

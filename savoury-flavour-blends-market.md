<p><p>The savoury flavour blends market encompasses various types, including natural flavours, natural flavours with other natural flavours (WONF), natural and artificial flavours, and synthetic flavours. Natural flavours derive from plant or animal sources, providing authentic taste. WONF blends enhance the flavour profile using a mix of natural components without revealing specific sources. Natural and artificial flavours combine both, offering versatility. Synthetic flavours are chemically formulated to mimic natural tastes, often providing cost-effective solutions for manufacturers while maintaining desired flavour profiles.</p></p>

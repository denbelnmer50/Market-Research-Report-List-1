<p><ul><li>Plate Anemometers</li><li>Tube Anemometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure anemometer market comprises various types, primarily plate and tube anemometers. Plate anemometers utilize a flat plate to measure wind speed by assessing pressure differences created by airflow around the plate. Conversely, tube anemometers employ a cylindrical design, capturing airflow through a tube to determine velocity based on pressure changes inside the tube. Both types are essential in various applications, including meteorology, HVAC systems, and environmental monitoring, providing critical data for effective air movement analysis.</p></p>

<p><p>The graphite heat exchanger market includes three primary types: shell and tube, block, and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube exchangers consist of multiple tubes housed within a shell, ideal for high-pressure applications. Block exchangers feature tightly packed channels, optimizing space and enhancing heat transfer efficiency. Plate heat exchangers consist of numerous thin plates, providing a compact design and versatility in various applications. Each type is designed for specific operational needs, ensuring effective thermal management across industries.</p></p>

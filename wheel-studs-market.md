<p><ul><li>Screw-in Wheel Studs</li><li>Press-in Wheel Studs</li><li>Welded-in Wheel Studs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel studs market comprises three key types: screw-in, press-in, and welded-in wheel studs. Screw-in wheel studs are threaded and can be easily replaced, offering convenience for maintenance. Press-in wheel studs are designed for permanent installation, providing strong fixation in the wheel hub. Welded-in wheel studs are securely attached through welding, ensuring maximum durability and resistance to loosening. Each type serves distinct applications in automotive and industrial sectors, catering to different assembly and performance requirements.</p></p>

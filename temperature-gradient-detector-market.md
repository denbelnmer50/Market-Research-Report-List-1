<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature gradient detector market is segmented into contact and contactless types. Contact detectors directly measure temperature by physically touching the object, offering precise readings but potentially affecting the object's thermal state. In contrast, contactless detectors utilize infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, allowing for safe and non-invasive assessments. Each type serves different applications; contact detectors are ideal for industrial settings, while contactless options are preferred in scenarios where object integrity is crucial.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>NK Cell Therapies</li><li>NK Cell Directed Antibodies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Natural Killer (NK) Cell Therapeutics market comprises two main types: NK Cell Therapies and NK Cell Directed Antibodies. NK Cell Therapies involve the use of activated NK cells to target and kill cancer cells, enhancing the immune response. These therapies can be autologous (from the patient) or allogeneic (from a donor). NK Cell Directed Antibodies are designed to bind specifically to cancer cells, enhancing NK cell activity and inducing targeted immune responses against tumors. Both approaches aim to improve cancer treatment outcomes.</p></p>

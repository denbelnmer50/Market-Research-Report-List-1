<p><p>The electronic discovery (e-discovery) software market is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based e-discovery software is hosted on remote servers, providing users with scalability, flexibility, and easier collaboration, as it can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection. Conversely, on-premise e-discovery software is installed and run on local servers, offering organizations greater control over their data and compliance, but requiring more IT resources for maintenance and upgrades. Both types cater to varying business needs and preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>The rigid-flex board market comprises three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multi-layer boards. Single-sided rigid-flex boards feature one conductive layer, suitable for simpler applications where space is limited. Double-sided boards include conductors on both sides, offering increased complexity and functionality. Multi-layer boards, on the other hand, consist of several layers of rigid and flexible materials, enabling high-density interconnections and advanced design capabilities. These variants cater to diverse applications, from consumer electronics to aerospace and medical devices.</p></p>

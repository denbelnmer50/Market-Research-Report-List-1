<p><p>The Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) market is divided into two main types: rigid containers and flexible bulk containers. Rigid containers are constructed from durable materials like plastic, steel, or composite materials, providing stability and protection for bulk liquids and solids. Flexible bulk containers, often made from woven fabric, are lightweight and ideal for transporting dry materials. Both types cater to diverse industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals, emphasizing efficient storage, transport, and reduced material handling costs.</p></p>

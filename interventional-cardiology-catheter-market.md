<p><p>The Interventional Cardiology Catheter Market includes various surgical approaches for heart procedures. Open-heart surgery involves directly accessing the heart through a large incision, allowing for complex repairs but requiring significant recovery time. Off-pump heart surgery, on the other hand, also utilizes an incision but avoids the use of a heart-lung machine, enabling quicker recovery. Minimally invasive heart surgery employs small incisions and advanced techniques, resulting in reduced pain and shorter hospital stays, making it increasingly popular among patients.</p></p>

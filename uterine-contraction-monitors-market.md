<p><strong>The Uterine Contraction Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Uterine Contraction Monitors</li><li>External Uterine Contraction Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uterine contraction monitors market is segmented primarily into internal and external types. Internal uterine contraction monitors involve the insertion of a catheter directly into the uterus, allowing for precise and continuous measurement of contractions and intrauterine pressure. In contrast, external uterine contraction monitors are non-invasive devices placed on the abdomen to measure contractions using sensors. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, with internal monitors providing detailed insights during labor, while external monitors offer ease of use and patient comfort.</p></p>

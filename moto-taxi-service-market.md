<p><p>Moto taxi services primarily operate within the e-hailing and ride-sharing market segments. E-hailing facilitates on-demand bookings via mobile apps, allowing passengers to request motorcycle taxis instantly. In contrast, ride-sharing involves multiple passengers sharing a single ride, typically reducing costs and optimizing vehicle use. Both models enhance urban mobility, offering convenience and efficiency, but differ in their operational structure—e-hailing focuses on individual rides, while ride-sharing emphasizes collaborative travel among users heading in similar directions.</p></p>

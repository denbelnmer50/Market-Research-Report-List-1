<p><strong>The Automotive Tire Pressure Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Tire Pressure Monitoring System</li><li>Direct Tire Pressure Monitoring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market is divided into two main types: Indirect TPMS and Direct TPMS. Indirect TPMS uses existing wheel speed sensors to estimate tire pressure based on changes in wheel rotation, alerting drivers to potential under-inflation. In contrast, Direct TPMS employs pressure sensors placed within each tire, providing precise real-time pressure readings. While Indirect TPMS is generally more cost-effective, Direct TPMS offers enhanced accuracy and immediate alerts, influencing consumer preference and market trends.</p></p>

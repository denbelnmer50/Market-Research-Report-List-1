<p><p>The heavy-duty pumps market encompasses various types, notably hydraulic pumps and pneumatic pumps. Hydraulic pumps utilize pressurized fluid to transmit energy and are ideal for high-pressure applications, offering robust performance in construction and industrial settings. In contrast, pneumatic pumps operate using compressed air, making them suitable for transferring liquids efficiently and safely in environments where electrical ignition risks are a concern. Both types play crucial roles in diverse sectors, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability in heavy-duty applications.</p></p>

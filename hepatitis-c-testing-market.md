<p><p>The Hepatitis C testing market is primarily categorized into three types: testing for the virus, antibodies, and antigens. Virus testing detects the presence of the Hepatitis C virus (HCV) directly, typically through viral load tests. Antibody testing identifies antibodies produced in response to HCV infection, indicating past or current infection. Antigen testing detects specific proteins from the virus, providing earlier diagnosis than antibody tests. Each testing type plays a crucial role in diagnosing and managing Hepatitis C.</p></p>

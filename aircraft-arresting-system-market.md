<p><p>The Aircraft Arresting System market encompasses various types designed to safely stop aircraft during emergencies. Net barriers consist of flexible nets that capture and decelerate aircraft. Cable systems use heavy cables for rapid deceleration. Mobile Aircraft Arresting Systems (MAAS) are portable solutions for diverse locations. Engineered Material Arresting Systems (EMAS) utilize crushable materials to slow aircraft with minimal damage. The Aircraft Carrier Arresting System employs cables and hydraulic systems to secure landing on carriers, ensuring aviation safety.</p></p>

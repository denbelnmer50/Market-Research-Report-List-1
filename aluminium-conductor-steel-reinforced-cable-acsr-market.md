<p><p>Aluminium Conductor Steel-Reinforced (ACSR) cables are widely used in overhead power transmission. The market includes various types: ACSR features an aluminum conductor reinforced with steel for strength; ACSR/AW incorporates aluminum-clad steel for improved corrosion resistance; and ACSR/TW uses trapezoidal aluminum conductors to enhance conductivity and reduce resistance. Each type caters to specific needs in electrical infrastructure, balancing factors like weight, strength, and conductivity to optimize performance in various environmental conditions.</p></p>

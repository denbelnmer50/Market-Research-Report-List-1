<p><ul><li>Self-contained</li><li>Remote Condensing</li><li>Multiplex Condensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The walk-in coolers and freezers market consists of three primary types: self-contained, remote condensing, and multiplex condensing systems. Self-contained units have integrated refrigeration components, making them easy to install and ideal for smaller spaces. Remote condensing systems feature compressors located away from the storage area, enhancing energy efficiency and noise reduction. Multiplex condensing units combine multiple refrigeration systems into one, providing consistent cooling while optimizing energy use and reducing operational costs, suitable for larger commercial applications.</p></p>

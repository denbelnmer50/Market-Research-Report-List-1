<p><ul><li>Non-Conductive Type</li><li>Conductive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Die Attach Film market is categorized into two primary types: Non-Conductive and Conductive. Non-Conductive Die Attach Films are used to bond semiconductor devices without electrical connectivity, making them ideal for applications requiring insulation and high thermal resistance. In contrast, Conductive Die Attach Films facilitate electrical connections while also providing adhesion, which is crucial for devices needing both thermal management and electrical performance. Each type serves specific applications in electronics, influencing design choices in semiconductor packaging.</p></p>

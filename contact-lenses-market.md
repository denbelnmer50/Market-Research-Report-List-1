<p><p>The contact lenses market comprises three main types: rigid contact lenses, soft contact lenses, and hybrid contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses offer durability and crisp vision, suitable for various corneal conditions. Soft contact lenses, made from flexible materials, provide comfort and ease of use, appealing to a broad consumer base. Hybrid contact lenses combine the benefits of both types, featuring a rigid center for optical clarity surrounded by a soft outer skirt for comfort, effectively catering to diverse user preferences.</p></p>

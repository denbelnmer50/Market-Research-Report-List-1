<p><strong>The Bone Graft Harvesting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cancellous Bone Harvesting</li><li>Marrow Harvesting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Graft Harvesting System market comprises two primary types: cancellous bone harvesting and marrow harvesting. Cancellous bone harvesting focuses on collecting spongy bone tissue, typically from areas like the iliac crest, which provides excellent osteoconductive properties for grafting. In contrast, marrow harvesting involves extracting bone marrow, rich in hematopoietic and mesenchymal stem cells, crucial for regeneration and repair. Both methods are integral to orthopedic and reconstructive surgeries, facilitating enhanced patient outcomes through effective bone grafting solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>The neonatal simulator market can be divided into two main types: tethered and tetherless simulators. Tethered simulators are connected to external devices, providing real-time data and comprehensive monitoring. They offer advanced functionalities but limit mobility and can complicate certain training scenarios. In contrast, tetherless simulators operate independently, allowing for greater flexibility and realism in simulations. They are often battery-operated and provide a more immersive experience, making them suitable for various training environments and mobile learning scenarios.</p></p>

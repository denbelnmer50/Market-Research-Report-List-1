<p><p>Cartridge valves are critical components in hydraulic systems, classified mainly into screw-in and slip-in types. Screw-in cartridge valves are designed for direct threading into a manifold, providing a compact solution with high pressure ratings. They offer ease of replacement and versatility in application. Slip-in cartridge valves, meanwhile, slide into a dedicated pocket within the manifold, allowing for quick installation and maintenance. Both types enhance system efficiency and reliability, catering to diverse industrial and mobile equipment needs.</p></p>

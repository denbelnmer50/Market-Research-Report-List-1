<p><ul><li>Magnetic Stationary Cycle</li><li>Electromagnetic Stationary Cycle</li><li>Self-generation Stationary Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary cycles market encompasses three primary types: magnetic, electromagnetic, and self-generating stationary cycles. Magnetic stationary cycles use magnets to create resistance, offering a smooth and quiet ride, ideal for home use. Electromagnetic cycles utilize electronically controlled resistance, allowing for precise adjustments and varied workout intensities. Self-generating cycles harness the rider's energy to power the electronic components, promoting eco-friendliness while eliminating the need for external power sources. Each type caters to different user preferences, fitness levels, and workout environments.</p></p>

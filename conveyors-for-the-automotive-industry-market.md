<p><p>Conveyors in the automotive industry play a crucial role in materials handling and assembly processes. Chain conveyors use linked chains for heavy loads and long distances. Belt conveyors consist of a continuous loop of material for smooth transport of lighter items. Hinged conveyors utilize metal plates for versatile movement of parts, while orbital conveyors offer circular motion, ideal for compact spaces. Other types may include roller conveyors or pneumatic systems, catering to specific manufacturing needs and enhancing efficiency in automotive production.</p></p>

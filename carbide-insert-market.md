<p><p>The carbide insert market features various coating types enhancing performance and durability. TiN coatings provide a hard, wear-resistant surface, improving tool life. TiC coatings offer superior hardness and thermal stability, ideal for heavy machining. Ti(C)N coatings combine properties of both titanium carbide and nitride, enhancing toughness and oxidation resistance. TiAlN coatings allow for high-temperature applications with improved hardness and oxidation resistance. Each coating type is tailored for specific machining applications, optimizing efficiency and performance in cutting tools.</p></p>

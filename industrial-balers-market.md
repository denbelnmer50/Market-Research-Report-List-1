<p><p>The industrial balers market is primarily segmented into vertical and horizontal balers. Vertical balers are compact, space-saving machines ideal for small to medium volumes of recyclables, offering an efficient way to create dense bales. In contrast, horizontal balers are larger and designed for high-volume operations, processing diverse materials such as cardboard and plastics. They feature automatic feeding and discharge mechanisms, making them suitable for large-scale facilities. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing waste management and recycling efforts.</p></p>

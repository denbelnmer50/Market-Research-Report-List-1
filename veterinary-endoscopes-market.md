<p><p>The veterinary endoscopes market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible endoscopes. Rigid veterinary endoscopes are designed for specific procedures and provide high visualization quality, often used in surgeries or examinations where a fixed angle is sufficient. In contrast, flexible veterinary endoscopes offer greater maneuverability and adaptability for reaching various body cavities, making them ideal for diagnostic procedures in animals. Both types are essential tools in veterinary medicine for minimally invasive diagnostics and treatment options.</p></p>

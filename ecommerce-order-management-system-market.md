<p><p>Ecommerce Order Management Systems (OMS) are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based OMS operate on remote servers, allowing businesses to access their systems online, offering scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises OMS are installed locally on company servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type has its own advantages, and the choice depends on a business's specific needs, budget, and operational capabilities.</p></p>

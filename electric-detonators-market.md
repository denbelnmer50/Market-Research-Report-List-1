<p><strong>The Electric Detonators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>lnstantaneous Electric Detonators</li><li>Delay Electric Detonators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric detonators market is divided into two primary types: instantaneous electric detonators and delay electric detonators. Instantaneous electric detonators are designed to explode immediately upon receiving an electric signal, providing precise timing for immediate detonation. In contrast, delay electric detonators are engineered to introduce a controlled delay between the triggering signal and the explosion, enabling sequential detonations. This delay enhances safety and efficiency in applications such as mining, construction, and demolition, by allowing for better management of shock waves and project timing.</p></p>

<p><p>Multi-Axial Simulation Table (MAST) systems are utilized for testing the dynamic responses of materials and structures under various loading conditions. The market is primarily divided into two types: electric simulation tables and hydraulic simulation tables. Electric simulation tables offer precise control and fast response times, making them suitable for small-scale testing applications. Hydraulic simulation tables provide higher force capacities, ideal for large-scale tests, and are often used in civil engineering and seismic research, enabling comprehensive material behavior analysis.</p></p>

<p><p>The air conditioner cleaner market features primarily two types: spray and smear. Spray type cleaners are convenient and widely used, allowing for even distribution and easy application on coils and filters. They quickly dissolve dirt and grime without the need for extensive scrubbing. On the other hand, smear type cleaners are thicker, often gel-like, and are applied directly to surfaces, providing a more targeted cleaning. Each type caters to different user preferences and cleaning needs in maintaining air conditioning efficiency.</p></p>

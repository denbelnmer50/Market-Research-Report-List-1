<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Diamond Compound</li><li>Oil-soluble Diamond Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond grinding paste market features two primary types based on their solubility: water-soluble and oil-soluble diamond compounds. Water-soluble diamond compounds are ideal for applications requiring easy cleanup and are often used in industries like electronics and medical device manufacturing. In contrast, oil-soluble diamond compounds provide enhanced lubrication, making them suitable for heavy-duty grinding tasks in metalworking. Both types are essential for precision machining and surface finishing, catering to different operational requirements and environments.</p></p>

Radio frequency filters eliminate unwanted frequencies to enhance signal quality, and low noise amplifiers improve signal clarity by minimizing noise. Duplexers facilitate simultaneous transmission and reception on a single channel, optimizing efficiency. Other market types may include mixers, attenuators, and couplers, all contributing to the overall performance and reliability of RF systems across multiple applications.</p></p>

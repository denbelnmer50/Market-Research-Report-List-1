<p><p>The motor driver board market primarily consists of H-Bridge motor driver boards and dual H-Bridge motor driver boards. H-Bridge motor driver boards allow for the control of a single DC motor's direction and speed by enabling voltage to be applied in both forward and reverse directions. Dual H-Bridge motor driver boards, on the other hand, can manage two motors simultaneously, providing enhanced control for applications requiring multiple motors, such as robotics and automated systems, thus expanding their usability in various electronic projects.</p></p>

<p><p>The life vests market consists of three main types: foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests offer inherent buoyancy and durability, making them suitable for various water activities. Inflatable vests are lightweight and compact, providing buoyancy only when inflated, ideal for recreational use. Hybrid vests combine features of both, offering the comfort of inflatable designs with the additional safety of foam. Each type caters to different needs, preferences, and safety regulations for users in aquatic environments.</p></p>

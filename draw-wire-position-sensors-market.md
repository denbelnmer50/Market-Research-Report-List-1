<p><p>Draw Wire Position Sensors, also known as string pots or cable displacement sensors, are devices that measure linear displacement by utilizing a wire or cable that is wound around a spool. As the target object moves, the wire unwinds or rewinds, transforming the linear movement into an electrical signal that can be measured. These sensors are widely used in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, manufacturing, and automation, due to their accuracy and reliability in monitoring position and movement.</p><p>The Draw Wire Position Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period.

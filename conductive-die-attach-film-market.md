<p><p>The Conductive Die Attach Film market is categorized into two main types: electro-conductive films and non-electro-conductive films. Electro-conductive films facilitate electrical connections and are essential for applications like semiconductor packaging, enabling efficient thermal management and electrical conductivity. In contrast, non-electro-conductive films serve as thermal interface materials without permitting electrical conduction, making them suitable for devices where electrical isolation is crucial. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics and automotive, addressing specific performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
